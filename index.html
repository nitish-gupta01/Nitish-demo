About The Project

Turning Sales Data Into Business Insights

Project Objective

The objective of this project is to transform sales data into a clear and interactive analytical dashboard. The dashboard helps users evaluate revenue, profitability, product performance, regional sales and historical sales trends from one centralized view.

Dashboard Approach

The project focuses on presenting complex sales information through KPI cards, category comparisons, sub-category analysis, geographical visualization, interactive filters and yearly trend analysis.

Data Insights

Key Findings From The Dashboard

The visualization reveals several useful patterns across sales, profit, product categories and yearly performance.

01

Office Supplies Leads Profit

Office Supplies generates the highest profit among the major product categories displayed in the dashboard.

02

Strong Sub-Category Performance

Binders and Art appear among the strongest profit-generating sub-categories in the analysis.

03

Sales Peaked in 2016

The yearly sales trend shows a significant increase through 2016, reaching the highest point in the displayed period.

04

Regional Performance Tracking

Regional sales visualization makes it possible to compare geographic performance and understand where sales are being generated.

Dashboard Features

Interactive Analysis

KPI Tracking

Uses map visualization to explore sales and profit information across different locations.

Displays historical sales movement across multiple years to identify growth and decline patterns.

Interactive Filters

Region, category and order-date filters allow users to dynamically explore specific portions of the data.
